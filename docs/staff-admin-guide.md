# Staff & Admin Guide

## Dashboard Access
Access the web dashboard at: `[Your-Web-App-URL]`

## 1. Staff Management (Admins Only)
*   Navigate to **Staff**.
*   **Add Staff**: Click "Add Staff", enter email, name, and role.
    *   **Homeroom**: Assign a class if the staff member is a homeroom teacher.
*   **Remove Staff**: Click "Remove" next to a staff member.
*   **Note**: Emails are case-insensitive.

## 2. Student Management
*   Navigate to **Students**.
*   **Import**: Upload a CSV with columns: `name`, `class`, `email`.
*   **Device Reset**:
    *   Find the student in the list.
    *   Click **Reset Device** if they lost their phone or got a new one.
    *   **View History**: Click "History" to see past resets.
    *   *Note: Only Admins or the student's Homeroom Teacher can reset devices.*

## 3. Flagged Check-ins
*   Navigate to **Flagged**.
*   Review check-ins flagged for:
    *   **Device Mismatch**: Student used a phone not bound to them.
    *   **Shared Device**: Multiple students used the same phone.
    *   **Location**: Check-in was far from the school coordinates.
*   **Actions**:
    *   **Dismiss**: False alarm.
    *   **Escalate**: Report to administration.

## 4. Attendance Reports
*   Navigate to **Reports**.
*   View daily summaries or export data for specific classes.
