# Student App Installation Guide

## Android Installation

1.  **Download the APK**: Obtain the `app-release.apk` file from your homeroom teacher or the school portal.
2.  **Enable Unknown Sources**:
    *   Go to **Settings > Security**.
    *   Enable **"Install unknown apps"** for the browser or file manager you are using.
3.  **Install**:
    *   Open the downloaded APK file.
    *   Tap **Install**.
4.  **Login**:
    *   Open the "Detta" app.
    *   Log in with your school email and password.
    *   **Important**: The first device you log in with will be bound to your account. You cannot use another device to check in unless a teacher resets it.

## iOS Installation (TestFlight)

*Note: iOS installation currently requires an invitation to TestFlight.*

1.  Download **TestFlight** from the App Store.
2.  Accept the email invitation from "Detta School App".
3.  Tap **Install** in TestFlight.

## Troubleshooting

*   **"App not installed"**: Ensure you have uninstalled any previous versions of the app.
*   **"Login Failed"**: Check your internet connection. If the issue persists, contact IT support.
*   **"Device Binding Error"**: You are trying to log in from a new device. Ask your homeroom teacher to reset your device binding.
